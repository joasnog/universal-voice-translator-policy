Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 23, 2025

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Universal Voice Translator is designed to function entirely offline and does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information.

Universal Voice Translator may request access to your device's microphone for voice input and playback functionality. This access is used solely for enabling the app's translation and conversation features. No audio or data is stored or transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with any third-party services or platforms that could collect your data.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
